NEWTON NGUYEN-LE
WORK ABOUT

I am Newton Nguyen-Le, a photographer and design student in Vancouver, BC. I am currently studying at the School of Interactive Art & Technology at Simon Fraser University.

Through my passion for photography and design, I can express my creative side and share my experiences with the world.

My photography seeks to capture the different qualities and personalities of a person and all the beautiful and memorable experiences I encounter in life. As a design student, I aim to create work that will visually enhance other's experiences. I am interested in work that allows me to share my experiences to further grow as a person and as an artist.
